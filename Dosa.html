Collection of different types of BreakFast Recipees

List of Dosa items

Plain Dosa

It is a crepe-like thin pancake made of rice and urad dal batter.
The batter is fermented overnight, which gives
it excellent flavour and texture.

Masala Dosa

Masala dosa has been India's most popular type of dosa for generations.
Masala dosa is often served with coconut chutney, sambar, and tomato ketchup.

Ravva Dosa

The batter is thin and watery, which makes the dosa crispy.
This dosa is a quick and easy option for breakfast
or when you have guests over.

Onion Dosa

Onion dosa is a simple yet flavorful dosa that is easy to make.
Onion dosa is crispy on the outside and soft and fluffy on the inside.

Pesarattu Dosa

Pesarattu dosa is a dosa made from green gram or moong dal.
It's a healthy and nutritious dish that is rich in protein.

Egg Dosa

Egg dosa is a delightful variation of the traditional South Indian
dosa, incorporating eggs for added protein and flavor.

List of other BreakFast items

Poori

Poori (or puri) is a popular deep-fried Indian bread known for
its puffed, golden-brown appearance and delicious taste.

Chapathi

Chapathi, also known as chapati or roti, is a staple Indian
flatbread made from whole wheat flour.

Parota

is a layered Indian flatbread known for its flaky and buttery texture. It is a
popular dish in South Indian cuisine, particularly in Kerala and Tamil Nadu.

Idli Vada

Idli and vada are classic South Indian breakfast items that
are both nutritious and delicious.
